Pizza

Description

This rad pizza is definitely not full of mutagen.

Ingredients

  • Mutagen
  • Crust
  • 4 pounds of grated cheese mix
  • Yes, more tomato sauce
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 325 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Put sauce on crust
  3. Put cheese on sauce (yes, all of the cheese)
  4. Put pepperonis on the cheese
  5. Don't forget the mutagen!
  6. Bake in oven for 10 minutes
  7. Cowabunga!
