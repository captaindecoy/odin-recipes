Lasagna

Description

This delicious dish will make your mouth water as you bemoan Mondays.

Ingredients

  • One entire pasta
  • One slice of swiss cheese
  • A can of tomato sauce
  • And one chubby cat

Steps

  1. Set oven to 4,000 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Throw all the ingredients in a big ol' dish
  3. Wait for 45-60 minutes. Then wait longer as it is probably not done. Keep waiting.
